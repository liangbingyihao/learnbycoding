笑话解释：It gets toad away

liangbing
 2023-11-05  35 words   One minute 
内容导航

笑话

Q: What happens to a frog’s car when it breaks down? A: It gets toad away.

解释

“bill” can refer to both a bird’s beak and a bill for payment.

“toad”（蟾蜍）与"towed"（拖走）sound similar in pronunciation.
