### 笑话
Q: What happens to a frog's car when it breaks down? 
A: It gets toad away.

### 解释

"bill" can refer to both a bird's beak and a bill for payment.

"toad"（蟾蜍）与"towed"（拖走）sound similar in pronunciation.
